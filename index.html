Track Cash - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 30, 2026
Your Privacy Matters.
Track Cash helps users manage personal finances while keeping financial information stored locally on their device. The app may display advertisements through CAS.AI and its advertising partners.

This Privacy Policy describes how Track Cash ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Track Cash is designed to store your financial information locally on your device.

We do not directly collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Home Address
  • Bank Credentials
  • Credit Card Information

2. Local Data Storage

Information entered into the App, including expenses, income, categories, budgets, notes, and settings, is stored locally on your device.

We do not upload your financial records to our servers.

3. Backup and Export

The App may provide backup, restore, export, or import features.

Any backup files created remain under your control and are stored on your device unless you manually transfer them elsewhere.

4. Advertising

Track Cash displays advertisements provided through CAS.AI and its advertising demand partners.

Advertising providers may automatically collect certain information to deliver ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and improve advertising services.

  • Advertising Identifiers
  • Device Information
  • IP Address
  • Approximate Location
  • App Interaction Information
  • Diagnostic Information

Such information is processed by advertising partners according to their own privacy policies.

5. Permissions

  • Notifications: Used for reminders and alerts created by you.
  • Storage Access: Used for exporting, importing, backup, and restore functions.
  • Internet Access: Used to load advertisements and open external links.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party advertising and service providers.

  • CAS.AI Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Information processed by advertising partners is handled according to their own privacy practices and policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Track Cash is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information stored within the App. However, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

10. Your Rights

  • Access your data directly within the App.
  • Delete your data by clearing app storage.
  • Export your information when supported.
  • Uninstall the App at any time.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Updates will be posted on this page and reflected by the Last Updated date shown above.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact information provided on our Google Play Store developer page.
